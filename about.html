About Us

Our Story

Delicious Takoyaki started as a small food stall in the heart of the city, bringing the authentic taste of Japan to our community. Our mission is to serve the best takoyaki using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes.


Mission and Values

Our mission is to deliver authentic, high-quality takoyaki that honors Japanese culinary traditions while providing a delightful and memorable experience for our customers.


Values

Authenticity : Using traditional recipes and techniques to create the most delicious takoyaki.

Quality: Sourcing the finest ingredients and maintaining the highest standards of preparation.

Respect: Honoring Japanese culinary heritage and treating our customers and employees with respect.

Community: Building a strong sense of community around our takoyaki and fostering a welcoming atmosphere.


Team Introduction

"Our team at Delicious Takoyaki is dedicated to bringing you the finest takoyaki experience. We are a passionate group of culinary professionals with years of experience in Japanese cuisine and food service. Leading our team is Lucian, a seasoned chef with a deep understanding of traditional takoyaki techniques. Licht oversees the kitchen, ensuring the highest quality ingredients and preparation methods. Our friendly front-of-house team, including Asta and Morgan, is committed to providing exceptional customer service and creating a welcoming atmosphere. Together, we strive to deliver an unforgettable takoyaki experience that will leave you wanting more."
